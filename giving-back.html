Our aligned relationships.

Realm of Caring

The Realm of Caring empowers “individuals, medical professionals, and the community through research-based education on hemp, CBD and medical cannabis, including THC.” We are proud to be a supported brand and one of only 4 brands to have received their Seal of Approval.

Hope Grows for Autisim

The mission at Hope Grows for Autism is to improve the lives of families affected by autism through research, education and advocacy of natural treatments. Research is showing that those with ASD (Autism Spectrum Disorder) have lower levels of anandamide. As CBDa interacts with the body’s endocannabinoid system, there is natural alignment.

1% for the Planet

1% for the Planet represents a global network of businesses, individuals and nonprofit organizations tackling our planet’s most pressing environmental issues. Sustainability drives everything we do at Planetarie. From our unique water-extraction process, to shopping as locally to minimizing waste, we do everything in our power to minimize our footprint.
