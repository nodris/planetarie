Our Story.

How we do for people, plants, and the planet.

“I believe deeply in the possibility of plant-based health, and I want to do everything I can to make natural human well-being a reality for as many people as possible.”

Stacy Cason - Founder and CEO of Planetarie

Background

“After 15 years as a medical provider, I saw first-hand the chronic pain epidemic in this country and the opioid addiction problem (The U.S. has 5% of the world's population and consumes 80% of opioids). Further, as a lifelong athlete, I needed something healthy and effective to reduce inflammation in my joints and enable more of the sports I love.

I am also a big believer in giving back. After a successful run with other business ventures, Planetarie has been a great medium to reach and help families with children with behavioral disorders and disabilities like epilepsy and autism.”

So why I started Planetarie?

1.

To bring a healthier, more natural & sustainable method for extracting cannabinoids, maintaining their raw stable state without harmful chemicals, solvents, or high pressure.

2.

To remove the stigma associated with this precious plant medicine, which has been wrongfully demonized as a "drug" for many years.

3.

To bring plant-based
healing to the mainstream.

“As a country girl with a diverse background, who believes promoting health is essential and is not afraid to stand up and say there is a better way, I invite you to learn more about all we do for people, plant, and the planet.”
